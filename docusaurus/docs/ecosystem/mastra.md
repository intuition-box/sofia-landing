# Mastra - Intelligent Agent Framework

Mastra provides the agent framework that powers Sofia's intelligence. These autonomous agents analyze your browsing patterns, extract meaningful insights, and help build your personal knowledge graph.

## Agent Capabilities

### Context Understanding

Agents understand the context of web pages you visit, categorizing content by topic, sentiment, and relevance.

### Pattern Recognition

Over time, agents learn your interests and behaviors, identifying patterns that define your digital identity.

### Signal Generation

Agents automatically generate signals (intuition triples) about your interests, skills, and preferences based on your activity.

### Recommendation Engine

Using your knowledge graph, agents suggest content, people, and opportunities aligned with your verified interests.

### Privacy by Design

All agent processing happens within the TEE, ensuring your raw data never leaves the secure environment.

## Multi-Agent Architecture

Sofia employs multiple specialized agents working together:

- **Content Analysis Agent** - Understands and categorizes web content
- **Social Connection Agent** - Maps relationships and network patterns
- **Skill Validation Agent** - Identifies and verifies professional competencies
- **Interest Mapping Agent** - Tracks and connects your interests over time
- **Recommendation Agent** - Generates personalized suggestions based on your graph

Each agent operates independently but shares insights through a secure communication protocol, creating a comprehensive understanding of your digital activity while respecting your privacy.


## Why Mastra for Sofia

Mastra's TypeScript-native approach aligns perfectly with Sofia's browser extension architecture, enabling:

- Seamless browser integration with shared TypeScript codebase
- Rapid agent development and iteration
- Production-grade security and observability
- Framework flexibility for future integrations
