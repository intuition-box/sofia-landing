# GaiaNet - Decentralized AI Infrastructure

GaiaNet is a decentralized AI infrastructure that powers Sofia's intelligent features. Unlike centralized AI services, GaiaNet distributes computation across a network of nodes, ensuring resilience, scalability, and privacy.

## Key Features

### Decentralized Model Serving

AI models run across multiple nodes, preventing single points of failure and ensuring availability.

### Privacy-Preserving Inference

When combined with Phala's TEE, GaiaNet enables private AI inference where your queries never expose sensitive information.

### Open Model Marketplace

GaiaNet supports multiple AI models, allowing Sofia to evolve and improve over time by integrating the best models for different tasks.

### Cost Efficiency

Decentralized infrastructure reduces costs compared to centralized AI services, making Sofia more sustainable and affordable.

## How Sofia Uses GaiaNet

Sofia leverages GaiaNet for:
- **Natural language processing** - Understanding the content and context of web pages
- **Semantic analysis** - Extracting meaning and relationships from your browsing data
- **Recommendation generation** - Suggesting relevant content and connections based on your interests
- **Pattern recognition** - Identifying trends and insights in your digital behavior

All processing happens within the secure TEE environment, ensuring your privacy is maintained throughout the AI pipeline.
