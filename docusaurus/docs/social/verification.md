# Social Verification

Link your social accounts to verify your identity and earn rewards.

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | Verification Method | Quest Reward |
|----------|--------------------| -------------|
| **Twitter/X** | OAuth connection | 100 XP |
| **Discord** | OAuth connection | 100 XP |
| **YouTube** | OAuth connection | 100 XP |
| **Twitch** | OAuth connection | 100 XP |
| **Spotify** | OAuth connection | 100 XP |

:::caution Alpha Limitation
Due to alpha-stage limitation, **Spotify** and **Twitter/X** verification is not yet available to all users. See [Known Issues](../known-issues/social-verification.md) for details.
:::

## Golden Profile

When you verify **all 5 platforms**, you unlock:
- **Green Border**: A distinctive green border on your avatar
- **Social Linked Quest**: +100 XP bonus
- **Enhanced Trust**: Higher credibility in the network
