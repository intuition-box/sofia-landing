# You Remain in Control of Your Data

Sofia is built for privacy and decentralization:

- **Sensitive data protected** – Your personal information is encrypted and secure
- **Personal AI** – Your AI agent belongs to you and operates under your control
- **Ability to disable tracking at any time** – Full control over data collection
- **Your data is not held by a large platform** (neither by us) – True decentralization
- **You decide what to share, with whom and for what purpose** – Granular privacy controls
