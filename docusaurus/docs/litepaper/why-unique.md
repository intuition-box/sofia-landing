# Why Sofia Is Unique

Unlike classic social networks or recommendation engines, Sofia does not guess who you are – it understands you.

It builds with you a rich, evolving and certified digital identity, based on what you actually do. Each piece of information becomes an element of trust, validated by others in a human, contextual and transparent network.

## Key Differentiators

- **Behavior-based, not declared** – Your profile reflects what you actually do
- **Verifiable credentials** – Skills and interests validated by the community
- **Semantic knowledge graph** – Connected data that evolves with you
- **Privacy-first design** – You own and control your data
- **Meritocratic governance** – Influence based on contribution, not wealth
