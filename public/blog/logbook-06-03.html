Logbook 06/03

This week we shipped the Vote Tab — a brand new section in Resonance where you can support or oppose curated claims and lists. We also made it easier for new users to get started with a guided first claim experience, and improved stability and performance across the app.

Vote Tab

  • New Vote tab in Resonance — browse curated claims and lists, then take a position: support or oppose
  • Clear color-coded buttons so you always know which side you're backing
  • Expandable lists — tap to reveal all entries without leaving the page
  • See both sides of the bonding curve at a glance, along with how many people have staked
  • Track your own position on every claim
  • XP animation plays when you successfully stake — satisfying feedback for every vote
  • Fully redesigned layout and color palette for a cleaner, more readable experience

Getting Started: First Claim

  • A new guided modal walks you through your very first on-chain claim step by step
  • Works seamlessly whether you sign in with a wallet or through social login

Wallet Reliability

  • Fixed timeout issues that could occur when connecting your wallet
  • Connections are now faster and more stable overall

Support/Oppose in Circle Feed

  • You can now support or oppose directly from your circle feed
  • Fixed a visual glitch where the support button could stay highlighted after voting

Trending Improvements

  • More accurate certifier counts on trending claims
  • Better grouping of trends by domain for easier browsing

General Polish

  • Smoother loading experience across every page and tab
  • Fixed the Intuition status display in Settings
  • Better spacing and visual consistency throughout the app
  • Improved quest detection for social login users
