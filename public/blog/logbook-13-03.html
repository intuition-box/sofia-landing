Logbook 13/03

This week we shipped the Position Board — after certifying a page, you now see exactly who else certified it, their rank, and their avatar. We also added a Share to X button so you can tweet your certifications directly, moved streaks fully on-chain, and released Sofia Extension v0.3.0.

Position Board

  • After certifying a page, you now see a leaderboard of everyone who certified the same page, ranked by discovery order: Pioneer, Explorer, or Contributor
  • Each certifier is displayed with their ENS name and avatar — real identities instead of wallet addresses
  • The board appears right after a successful certification, and is also visible when browsing any page to see who already staked on it
  • Compact or expanded view — get the quick summary or dive into the full ranking

Share Certifications to X

  • One-click Share to X button lets you tweet your certification right after staking
  • You stay in full control of the post before publishing

On-Chain Streaks

  • Your daily activity streaks are now verified directly from the blockchain — no more relying on local browser data
  • What you see in your Achievements tab is exactly what the chain records

Claim Card Redesign

  • Claim cards have been visually redesigned to show TRUST score, number of stakers, and market cap at a glance
  • New Profit & Loss display — see how your positions are performing directly on each card

Multiple Intention Selection

  • Cards in the Circle Feed are now grouped by intention for a clearer overview
  • When certifying, you can choose which intention you want to support

New Debate Claims

  • Fresh batch of Intuition community claims to vote on

Responsive Layout

  • The extension now adapts properly to narrow side panel widths — modals, success screens, and the feed all display correctly in compact mode

General Polish

  • App version now visible on Settings and Home pages
  • Smoother modal animations with blur effects
  • Various bug fixes and stability improvements

What's Next

  • We've identified a few bugs, particularly around level display in the Echoes tab and Gold attribution. Fixes are coming next week.
